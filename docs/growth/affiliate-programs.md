# Affiliate Program Registry

This document records every affiliate relationship ToolKno has or is pursuing. It exists to:

1. Ensure Constitution Rule 4 compliance — editorial verdicts must be independent of commission rates. This registry enables quarterly audits comparing verdict quality against commission rates.
2. Track program terms, payment schedules, and link formats.
3. Provide a single reference for anyone managing affiliate relationships or reviewing editorial content for conflicts.

## Quarterly Editorial/Revenue Audit

On the first day of each calendar quarter, the founder reviews this table:

| Product | Our editorial verdict | Commission rate | Conflict? |
|---|---|---|---|
| *(completed after first reviews are published)* | | | |

**Conflict definition:** A product with a high commission rate (> 30%) receives a better editorial verdict than comparable products with lower commission rates (< 15%), with no editorial justification for the discrepancy.

**If a conflict is found:** The editorial verdict is re-reviewed against the testing evidence. The commission rate does not change the verdict. If the verdict stands up to re-review, the audit result is documented. If the verdict was influenced by commission rate, it is corrected and the correction is documented.
