# Master Execution Plan

This document covers the 12-month execution roadmap for ToolKno. It is directional, not prescriptive — the 90-Day Plan in `docs/roadmap/90-day-plan.md` contains week-by-week operational detail. This document covers the strategic milestones and their rationale.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1–3)

**Goal:** Ship the newsletter infrastructure, produce the first editorial content, earn the first affiliate commission.

**Product milestones:**
- PRD-002 (Newsletter System) implemented and live
- `/methodology` page published before first review
- PRD-001 (Homepage) newsletter section live and converting
- PRD-011 (Affiliate Registry) internal tool live

**Editorial milestones:**
- First review published (Writing & Content Software niche — Grammarly or ProWritingAid)
- First newsletter issue sent to confirmed subscribers
- Methodology page published

**SEO milestones:**
- All existing tool pages verified at ≥ 9/10 SEO quality
- First editorial content indexed and ranking

**Revenue milestones:**
- First affiliate commission earned
- AdSense revenue baseline established

**Newsletter milestones:**
- First 100 confirmed subscribers
- First issue sent

**Gate criteria to proceed to Phase 2:**
- Newsletter system fully operational (confirmed subscribers, scheduled sends working)
- At least one review published and indexed
- At least one affiliate commission earned (proves conversion)

---

## Phase 2: Editorial Depth (Months 4–6)

**Goal:** Establish ToolKno as the most detailed review source in the Writing & Content Software niche. Build the editorial flywheel.

**Product milestones:**
- PRD-003 (Reviews) implemented
- PRD-004 (Comparison Engine) implemented
- PRD-012 (Methodology Page) finalized and promoted

**Editorial milestones:**
- 5–8 reviews published across writing tool category
- 3 head-to-head comparisons published (Grammarly vs. ProWritingAid, Hemingway vs. Grammarly, etc.)
- First buying guide published ("Best Grammar Checkers for Writers")
- Newsletter cadence: biweekly, consistent

**SEO milestones:**
- Review and comparison pages ranking for long-tail queries
- First editorial backlinks earned (via outreach + organic)
- Internal linking from tool pages to related editorial content

**Revenue milestones:**
- Affiliate revenue: $200–$500/month (expected)
- First newsletter sponsorship inquiry (respond only when list ≥ 1,500)

**Newsletter milestones:**
- 500 confirmed subscribers
- Consistent > 35% open rate
- Click rate > 8%

**Gate criteria to proceed to Phase 3:**
- At least 5 reviews and 3 comparisons published
- Newsletter at ≥ 500 confirmed subscribers
- Affiliate revenue > $200/month

---

## Phase 3: Niche Expansion (Months 7–12)

**Goal:** Expand into Creator Economy Tools niche while deepening Writing & Content coverage. Begin monetizing newsletter via sponsorships.

**Product milestones:**
- PRD-005 (Buying Guides) implemented
- PRD-006 (Software Database) implemented
- PRD-008 (Search) implemented
- PRD-009 (User Accounts) implemented

**Editorial milestones:**
- Total: 15–20 reviews published
- Creator Economy tools coverage begins (Beehiiv, ConvertKit, Kajabi, Ghost)
- 2–3 creator-focused buying guides published
- Newsletter positions ToolKno as the trusted source for creator software decisions

**SEO milestones:**
- Editorial pages contributing meaningful organic traffic
- "Best [category]" buying guides ranking in top 10 for primary keywords
- Site authority building through consistent editorial backlink acquisition

**Revenue milestones (Month 12 expected):**
- Affiliate revenue: $400–$2,000/month
- Newsletter sponsorship revenue: $0–$500/month (depends on subscriber count)
- Total: $400–$2,500/month

**Newsletter milestones (Month 12):**
- 1,500–2,500 confirmed subscribers
- Sponsorship offering active at ≥ 2,000 subscribers

## Editorial Roadmap

### Writing & Content Software (Months 1–6)

**Priority reviews:**
1. Grammarly — the market leader; highest search volume; clear affiliate program
2. ProWritingAid — strong alternative; dedicated audience; good affiliate rate
3. Hemingway Editor — free tier popular with our tool audience; no affiliate program but high trust-building value
4. QuillBot — used heavily by the academic/content writer audience
5. Jasper — AI writing; controversial category; honest review will earn attention
6. Writesonic / Copy.ai — AI alternatives; comparison opportunity

**Priority comparisons:**
1. Grammarly vs. ProWritingAid (highest search volume in category)
2. Grammarly vs. Hemingway (different use cases — grammar vs. clarity)
3. AI writing tools comparison (Jasper vs. Writesonic vs. Copy.ai)

**Priority guides:**
1. Best Grammar Checkers for Writers
2. Best AI Writing Assistants for Content Marketers
3. Grammarly vs. Free Alternatives

### Creator Economy Tools (Months 7–12)

**Priority reviews:**
1. Beehiiv — newsletter platform; 50% affiliate commission; growing fast
2. Ghost — publishing platform; strong developer and creator audience
3. ConvertKit (Kit) — email for creators; established audience
4. Kajabi — course + community platform; 30% lifetime affiliate
5. Notion — widely used by creators; comparison opportunity

## Newsletter Roadmap: 0 → 100K

This is a long-term directional plan, not a 12-month target.

| Milestone | What drives it | Estimated timeline |
|---|---|---|
| 0 → 100 subscribers | Converting existing tool users via homepage form | Month 1–2 |
| 100 → 500 subscribers | First editorial content + word of mouth + SEO | Month 2–4 |
| 500 → 1,000 subscribers | Consistent biweekly issues + referral from editorial readers | Month 4–6 |
| 1,000 → 2,500 subscribers | Creator Economy niche expansion + cross-promotion | Month 7–12 |
| 2,500 → 5,000 subscribers | Newsletter sponsorship deals (paid distribution) + SEO flywheel | Year 2 |
| 5,000 → 25,000 subscribers | Brand authority + referral program + guest editorial | Year 3 |
| 25,000 → 100,000 subscribers | Category authority + potential sponsored partnerships + paid acquisition if ROI-positive | Year 4–5 |

## Growth Flywheel

See `docs/vision/mission-and-vision.md` for the full flywheel diagram. The key insight: the flywheel self-reinforces once all components exist. The current state is one component (tools). The newsletter (PRD-002) adds the second. Reviews (PRD-003) add the third. At that point, the flywheel can turn.
